The Accomodation

We offer modern accommodation facilities, our semi-independent homes are ordinary domestic houses and they do not stand out as being different from other houses in the area.

From the inside, they are comfortably furnished to create a warm homely feel and have private meeting rooms where young people can meet with their parents and professionals.

* Images shown here are for illustration purposes only, actual accommodation may differ slightly

The Location

Our homes are ideally located: within walking distance of shops, schools, health and dental practices. Our locations offer a large range of local services for young people with numerous opportunities for leisure activities. In addition, every location has excellent public transport links with main line railway stations nearby.

The Experience

We provide young people with shared living experiences to prepare them for independent living, and for those young people that require additional support due to challenging behaviour and gang affiliation we offer 24 hour support, and 1:1 and 2:1 packages of care.
