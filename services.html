Our Staff and Support

Our programmes are adapted to the young person’s emotional and physical needs at any given time so that the young person can play a full, meaningful and appropriate part in their community and form constructive relationships with those around them. By providing a caring environment including welcoming accommodation, to understanding their motivations, supplying information and redirecting focus, staff ensure that young people obtain a better understanding of their circumstances and the world around them. With a clearer understanding of the options available, young people can make informed choices about their housing, educational and employment needs.

All ASG ltd Staff are fully trained and hold Current Enhanced DBS certificates.

Our Services

1-to-1 Mentoring

Befriending, tangible goals setting, supervised outings, role modelling, life coaching, teaching responsibility, valuing traditional skills, conflict resolution, Anger management, identity, building strong solid foundations, exploring passions and expectations, strengthening family values and boundaries.

1-to-1 Sports Coaching

These mentoring sessions are delivered by qualified coaches who have been trained to work with young disaffected people and use sport to redirect their focus positively. This service can be delivered through sports we specialise in such Tennis/Badminton/Squash/Football/Boxing/martial arts. We also arrange participation in sports tournaments that promote social cohesion.

Drama, Arts, Film and Music production

This intervention is all about expression through role-playing exercises, music writing and production, art and short films and plays. This helps young people express themselves and produce work they can look back on. We have professional actors, artists and music producers, that have been trained to work with disaffected young people provide engaging sessions for the most hard to reach young people.

1-to-1 or Group Activities on Anti-Social Behaviour

This includes mentoring on alcohol, drugs, gang, anti-social behaviour, Anti CSE mentoring. Awareness of community issues, becoming active and responsible citizens, Sun Tzu training, building integrity, and ‘introspection’ as a way of looking inward, to identify challenges and create solutions.
